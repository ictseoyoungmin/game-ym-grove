# Ym Grove Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-18

Ym Grove stores game progress locally on the player's device. The app does not require an account, does not collect personal information, does not use ads, and does not send gameplay data to a server.

Stored local data may include resources, growth stats, unlocked Ym variants, selected Ym, and last saved time. Players can clear this data by clearing the app/browser storage or uninstalling the app.

Ym Grove does not request network access for the v0.1 Android build.
